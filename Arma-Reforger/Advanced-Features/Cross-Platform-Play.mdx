# Enabling Cross-Platform Play in Arma Reforger  

Cross-platform play allows players on different devices (e.g., PC and consoles) to join the same server. This guide will show you how to configure your server to support cross-platform play effectively.

## Overview  

Cross-platform play enhances accessibility and expands your player base. However, it requires careful configuration to ensure compatibility and fair gameplay.

---

## Steps to Enable Cross-Platform Play  

### Step 1: Update Server Settings  
Enable cross-platform play in the `server.cfg` file:  
```properties
# Enable cross-platform play
crossplay=true
```

---

### Step 2: Configure Input Settings  
Ensure input methods (e.g., keyboard/mouse vs. controller) are balanced:  
```properties
# Balance input methods
input-balancing=true
```

---

### Step 3: Test Cross-Platform Compatibility  
1. Invite players on different platforms to join the server.  
2. Monitor gameplay for issues like lag or unfair advantages.  

---

## Troubleshooting Common Issues  

### Issue: Players Cannot Connect  
- **Cause**: Firewall restrictions or incorrect settings.  
- **Solution**:  
  1. Open required ports for all platforms.  
  2. Verify that `crossplay=true` is enabled in `server.cfg`.  

### Issue: Unbalanced Gameplay  
- **Cause**: Differences in input methods or hardware capabilities.  
- **Solution**:  
  1. Adjust settings to balance input methods.  
  2. Gather feedback from players to identify imbalances.  

---

## Best Practices  

- Communicate cross-platform support clearly to players.  
- Regularly test cross-platform sessions for compatibility.  
- Address player feedback to improve fairness and accessibility.
